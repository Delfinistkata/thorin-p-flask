What is Airsoft?

Airsoft is a dynamic and adrenaline-fueled recreational sport that simulates military combat using replica firearms that shoot plastic BBs. It offers a thrilling and immersive experience for players of all ages and skill levels. From intense skirmishes to tactical team operations, Airsoft brings together elements of strategy, teamwork, and physical fitness.
